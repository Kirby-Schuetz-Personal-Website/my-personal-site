Bears

  1. Ollie
  2. Mona

Bears are not as unpredictable and dangerous as Hollywood or the media would have us believe. Ollie and Mona exhibit very predictable behaviour. This trait can be beneficial to people who come into contact with bears.

Giraffes

Giraffes form loosely organized groups of 2-20 animals. Female giraffes are more social and usually found in groups, especially our girls Frankie and Coconut!
