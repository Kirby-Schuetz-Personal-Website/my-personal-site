About

Kirby Schuetz grew up in Iowa. She spent her early years studying the arts, but then felt a pull towards education. In 2008. she graduated with a Bachelors of Education focusing in elementary education, reading, and art education. Schuetz worked as an educator at a low income elementary school for 5 years before deciding to make a career change. Her focus is now on software development in the hopes of being able to use her vast background to impact the tech world.
